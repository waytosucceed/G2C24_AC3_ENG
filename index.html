What did we learn today?

Reflexive pronouns are special words that we use when the person doing the action is also the person receiving the action.
These pronouns end in "-self" or "-selves."

Examples of Reflexive Pronouns:
Myself, Yourself, Himself, Herself, Itself, Ourselves, Yourselves, Themselves

"Self" is used when we are talking about just one person:
Myself, Yourself, Himself, Herself, Itself

We use "selves" when we are talking about more than one person:
Ourselves, Yourselves, Themselves
